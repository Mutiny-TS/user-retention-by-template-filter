Key Questions
What the data tells us
Question 1
Does using templates at all improve user retention?
Yes — by a significant margin. Users at companies that use templates come back at nearly 2.5× the rate of users at companies that don't. By Week 1, template-company users retained at 38.5% versus just 15.2% for non-template companies. By Week 2 that gap holds — 22.0% vs 6.5%. The pattern is consistent across every cohort in the heatmaps above.

Question 2
Does the amount of template usage correlate with retention?
Yes — more templates, more users coming back. Companies with zero templates saw only 19.6% of their orgs return week-over-week. Companies with 6–14 templates saw 87.5% return. The more embedded templates are in a company's workflow, the more their users keep showing up. The slight dip at 15+ is based on only 3 companies — too few to be conclusive.
